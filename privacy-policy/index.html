Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Chaowalit Greepoke ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use the Chaowalit Portfolio mobile application ("the App").

Information We Collect

The App is a portfolio showcase application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number) is collected
  • No location data is accessed or shared
  • No device identifiers are tracked
  • No analytics or crash reporting tools are used

Local Data Storage

The App stores your preferences locally on your device using SharedPreferences (Android) or equivalent local storage. This includes:

  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Language preference

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data. There are:

  • No advertising SDKs
  • No analytics SDKs (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No social media SDKs
  • No crash reporting tools

External Links

The App may contain links to external websites (GitHub, email, social media). When you click these links, you leave the App and are subject to the privacy policies of those external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party websites.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The App is a general-audience portfolio application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
